## Vibe check (nano-sized)

This week Google released its [Nano Banana](https://blog.google/products/gemini/updated-image-editing-model/) image editor, which promises quicker, smarter edits without the usual hassle and complexities of Adobe’s Photoshop. Here’s our initial take:
**Closer to replacing Photoshop (but not quite)**
“Yes, it's better than a lot of tools out there in terms of prompt adherence and granular localized edits. I think I'll find the real use cases when I'm jumping less into Photoshop.”*—****[Lucas Crespo](https://every.to/@lucascrespo)****, creative lead*

##### Understands the vibe, not just the edit

“I LOVED the speed and the image consistency, liked how it understood the [vibe of the image](https://x.com/anukshi13/status/1960544036902592857) and then made edits based on the vibe. I texted the same thing in ChatGPT, but the results weren’t as good.”*—****Anukshi Mittal****, project manager*

##### Brings out your inner artist

"I tried to use it as a painter. It's good at doing things step by step and keeping the previous images while just changing what you ask."—***[Kieran Klaassen](https://every.to/@kieran_1355)****, general manager of ****[Cora](http://cora.computer)***

## From Every Studio

##### Find your files with Sparkle Search

**“[Sparkle](https://makeitsparkle.co/?utm_source=everyfooter)** started as the best way to organize your files,” says **[Yash Poojary](https://every.to/@yashpoojary)**, the app's general manager. “But then came the question: ‘Okay, but where did Sparkle put them?’” This week, he and the team answered that question with the launch of Sparkle Search. The upgrade locates files significantly faster than macOS Spotlight, with no need to click through multiple Spotlight results or dig through multiple folders. Says Yash: “We’d love your [feedback](https://sparkle.featurebase.app/) to shape what comes next. Update to Sparkle 1.5.7 in your app or [download the latest release](https://github.com/EveryInc/sparkle-swift-build/releases/download/prod/Sparkle.dmg).”

##### Monologue's polish phase begins

Our AI dictation tool **[Monologue](https://monologue.to/)** is getting a visual upgrade with custom animations and a refined onboarding flow. **[Naveen Naidu Mummana](https://every.to/@naveen_6804)**, one of our entrepreneurs in residence, is collaborating with an animation expert to craft introductory animations that match the app's minimal aesthetic. Meanwhile, **Danny Aziz, **general manager of our writing assistant** **[Spiral](https://spiral.computer), is building an integration that will let you use Monologue to dictate text in Spiral instead of typing. Monologue is in [public beta](https://monologue.to/), with an official launch planned for this fall.
