Happy Sunday!

Not everyone will read this from the U.S. — that’s one of the great things about the internet. But that’s where we’re writing this from. And today, things look a little different than they did yesterday.

**Joe Biden and Kamala Harris are now President and Vice President-Elect,** and we would be remiss if we didn’t extend our heartfelt congratulations to them for their historic win. (And let you know that their [first](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xiLR4sCgvnc) [speeches](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xt8VvrdxzHo) were pretty hype.)

It was a rollercoaster week and it left us wondering — **did you get anything done?** And if so, what was your secret?

Everyone reacted to The News from different mental and emotional places, but whether you were infinitely scrolling, tuning everything out to stay focused, or simply dissociating altogether, we tried to put out something for everyone.

For the Stat Heads, or anyone with an opinion on [political betting](https://every.to/napkinmath/can-political-betting-markets-predict-17472500), a special guest wondered if bets could ever beat polls as a prediction model. If you tried to tune out the horse race and get productive, here’s a quick guide to [indistractibility](https://every.to/superorganizers/action-item-how-to-become-indistractable-18075418). And for those who just wanted to hear other people ride a mental seesaw: [Our podcasts](https://thelongconversation.substack.com/p/tlc-podcast-episode-2-the-functionhttps://thelongconversation.substack.com/p/tlc-podcast-3-ask-a-journalist-with) [have you](https://every.to/talktherapy/41-a-bad-situationcompounded-16837660) [covered](https://every.to/talktherapy/).

Wherever you were this week mentally and emotionally, now you’re here. Take a deep breath, put your feet up, and get ready to read.

#### How to reach a billion, Fast

It’s been two months since the launch of **Fast —** the online shopping startup that wants to bring one-click checkout to the world — and eight months since **Stripe** led the startup’s $20 million series A. This week, [The Information](https://www.theinformation.com/articles/fast-a-startup-backed-by-stripe-discusses-billion-dollar-valuation) reported that **Fast is eyeballing a second round…that could reach** **$1 billion**.

It’s still just a rumor, so you have to wonder where it’s coming from and why it got leaked. But assuming it’s true, that’s a pretty — quick — path to unicorn status even for a guy like Domm. It’s probably because of [the hoodies](https://swag.fast.co), right? [Read more.](https://www.theinformation.com/articles/fast-a-startup-backed-by-stripe-discusses-billion-dollar-valuation)
