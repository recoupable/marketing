Keeping up with AI is [Nathaniel Whittemore’s](https://x.com/nlw) full-time job—and I spent an hour with him to understand how he does it.

Nathaniel is the host of a top-ranked AI podcast on the technology charts, [The AI Daily Brief](https://www.youtube.com/@AIDailyBrief)*, *which breaks down the most important news in AI every day. He is also the founder and CEO of [Superintelligent](https://besuper.ai/), a platform that teaches you how to use AI for work and fun through interactive video tutorials.
