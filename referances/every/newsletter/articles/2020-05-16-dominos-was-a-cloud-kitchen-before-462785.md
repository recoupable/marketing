## 🔮 Dominance Friction

[Jesse Beyroutey](http://www.twitter.com/beyroutey), partner at IA ventures, has a simple formula for determining whether a company is going to be dominant: a company is most likely to be dominant when it sells a product that is maximally in line with its customer’s best interests. That simple maxim has a number of surprising implications, from how companies should price their products (price should be in line with costs), to how transparent they should be with their customers (as transparent as possible.) Don’t miss this one.
