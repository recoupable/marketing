# Deconstructing Social Tech, with Chris Messina

*And the product dynamics that drive social platforms *

For our guest this week on Means of Creation we are welcoming [Chris Messina](https://twitter.com/chrismessina)!
Chris is best known for[inventing the now ubiquitous hashtag](https://tedxbend.com/presenters/chris-messina/), but his work over the last 15 years spans social technology, product design, synthetic media, founder culture and mental fitness.
Currently, Chris helps founders and creators with their Product Hunt launches. His insight into social technology stems from his experience at big tech companies like Google and Uber, combined with his role in popularizing social innovations like coworking and unconferencing.
Chris has shared his unique perspective in popular Netflix documentary [The Social Dilemma](https://www.netflix.com/title/81254224), and in books like [No Filter](https://www.amazon.com/No-Filter-Inside-Story-Instagram/dp/1982126809) by Sarah Frier and[Billion Dollar Loser](https://www.hachettebookgroup.com/titles/reeves-wiedeman/billion-dollar-loser/9780316461368/) by Reeves Wiedeman.
In this interview, we talked to Chris about:

- How the **social technology landscape has changed** over the last few years

-
**Prominent trends **that nobody is talking about

- How founders should think about **technology vs social innovatio**n

- The commonalities shared by** successful Product Hunt launches**

- Product trends that have **accelerated during the pandemic**

- How Chris’ course [Nail Your Product Hunt Launch](https://producthunt.substack.com/about) has influenced his **perspective on EdTech**

- His experience **consulting on Superpeer**
