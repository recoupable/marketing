# #35 - Tricking Nat Eliason into joining the Bundle

Nat Eliason talks to Dan and Nathan about [Almanack](https://every.to/thealmanack/), his brand-new Bundle newsletter exploring everything it takes for you to live a life that is healthy, wealthy, and wise. Nat explains the appeal of joining the Bundle, how he got into the self-improvement world, and challenges Nathan to take his crown for the most-read Divinations post.
