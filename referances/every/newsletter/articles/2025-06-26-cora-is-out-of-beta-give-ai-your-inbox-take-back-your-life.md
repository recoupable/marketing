Your inbox is someone else's problem now.
Starting today, Cora—the AI chief of staff for your inbox—is coming out of private beta and is available for anyone to use. Cora manages email for over 2,500 beta users—now it's your turn.
[Try Cora](https://cora.computer/?source=post_button)
Here’s what Cora can do for you:

### Cora screens your emails

Cora knows what’s important to you and the types of emails you need to respond to. It keeps those messages in your inbox for you to handle ASAP.

### Cora pre-drafts responses in your voice

Whenever Cora has enough context to draft a great response for you, it does.
[![Uploaded image](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_cb5b7ac8-25dc-4d39-80d8-a3f05e0d15bf.png)](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_cb5b7ac8-25dc-4d39-80d8-a3f05e0d15bf.png)

### Cora briefs the rest

Twice a day, Cora sends you a beautiful Brief that summarizes everything you need to read but don’t need to respond to. It lets you read your inbox in 30 seconds instead of three hours.
[![Uploaded image](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_1efc85ee-1fe4-4fca-b23d-d1f37e311b08.png)](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_1efc85ee-1fe4-4fca-b23d-d1f37e311b08.png)

### Cora gets to know you, automatically

Cora reads your email patterns to discover who you are—your work, your style, and your priorities.

### You can shape Cora through conversation

Talk to Cora like your chief of staff over chat or email. Explain how you want emails filed and situations handled. You can even ask it to summarize or give an opinion on long threads or complex decisions. It remembers everything that matters.
[![Uploaded image](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_289e4dd5-c890-4fe2-a4a1-6ce36d542754.png)](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3684/optimized_289e4dd5-c890-4fe2-a4a1-6ce36d542754.png)

### Security and privacy are built in

Cora securely connects to your Gmail and works right alongside it—no app switching needed. We have no backdoor access or ability to view your emails. Period. Cora is also Google Verified and CASA Tier 2 compliant.
