# #47 - What founders can learn from Slack’s story

Reacting to the news of [Slack’s purchase by Salesforce](https://every.to/divinations/why-salesforce-bought-slack-21480730), Nathan gets into the different opportunities in hardware vs. software and Dan goes deep on building and wrestling with a co-browsing tool. Together, they plot how one might go about taking down Salesforce…if Salesforce doesn’t buy you first.
