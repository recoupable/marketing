*New York Times* bestselling author [Seth-Stephens Davidowitz](http://sethsd.com/) wrote a book in 30 days—and he did it with ChatGPT.

[Who Makes the NBA?](https://www.amazon.com/Who-Makes-NBA-Data-Driven-Basketballs-ebook/dp/B0CPW4VN28/ref=tmm_kin_swatch_0?_encoding=UTF8&qid=&sr=) is Seth’s third data science book. He challenged himself to write it in under a month after realizing how much faster he could work by teaming up with ChatGPT plugin [Advanced Data Analysis](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8437071-advanced-data-analysis-chatgpt-enterprise-version). Along the way, Seth discovered something else: AI made the process of writing this book way more fun. He handed off all the boring parts of data analysis, like writing code to generate charts from datasets, to AI. This freed him to focus on the part that he loves—thinking up questions designed to uncover insights from a dataset.

Seth is a data scientist, economist, and author of [Everybody Lies](https://www.harpercollins.com/products/everybody-lies-seth-stephens-davidowitz?variant=32123670822946)* *and [Don’t Trust Your Gut](https://www.harpercollins.com/products/dont-trust-your-gut-seth-stephens-davidowitz?variant=40685562069026). He’s worked at Google, lectured at [Wharton](https://www.wharton.upenn.edu/), and consulted for Fortune 500 companies.

[Who Makes the NBA?](https://www.amazon.com/Who-Makes-NBA-Data-Driven-Basketballs-ebook/dp/B0CPW4VN28/ref=tmm_kin_swatch_0?_encoding=UTF8&qid=&sr=) is a fascinating read, but it’s also an experiment in how AI is revolutionizing creative pursuits. In a world where AI is making every question that humans know the answer to immediately answerable, the real differentiator is asking the right questions—a skill that Seth isn’t just exceptionally good at, but also finds joy in.

I sat down with Seth to understand where AI sits in his data analysis process and how he used it to write a book in 30 days. We also prompt ChatGPT to answer an intriguing question of our own—which Olympic sport I’d be best at—live on the show.
