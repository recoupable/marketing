*Sunday, August 14, 2020*

Hello and happy Sunday!

As Nathan and Dan [discussed](https://every.to/talktherapy/19-what-should-we-do-with-our-weekly-842094) in Talk Therapy this week, we’re conducting an experiment with the Sunday Digest! It’s now a ranked list — so hopefully it will get you to the articles you want to read more quickly than ever!

#### What we published this week

We published** three new articles** this week **totaling ~6,000 words**. We dove into iOS hacks, how to get to inbox and to-do list zero, and the nitty gritty of infrastructure SaaS.
