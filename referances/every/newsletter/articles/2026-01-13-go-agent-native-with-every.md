*Learn to build with AI agents in two hands-on camps—plus get a free 30-day trial*

Over the past week, I’ve watched the team at Every get completely obsessed with this[agent-native](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/agent-native-architectures-how-to-build-apps-after-the-end-of-code) moment. **[Monologue](https://www.monologue.to/)** general manager **[Naveen Naidu](https://every.to/@naveen_6804)** and Every’s head of platform **Willie Williams** were vibe coding through the weekend, pushing agents to act as first-class citizens inside of apps—or, as **[Dan Shipper](https://every.to/@danshipper)** coined, to work like “Claude Code in a trench coat.” Naveen [built a read-later app](https://www.naveennaidu.com/writing/building-agent-native-read-later) in two hours that quickly understood his reading patterns better than he does. Dan himself built [Proof](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2010411900861813233), an agent-native markdown editor that tracks AI versus human writing, without writing any code. The non-engineers on the team are unleashing agents to help them do their jobs better, including strategy planning, copywriting, and building landing pages.
This is what it feels like to be inside a paradigm shift while it’s happening.
And the movement is growing. Thousands of Every subscribers are reading this[agent-native guide](https://every.to/guides/agent-native) in conversation with Claude or ChatGPT. Nearly 15,000 viewers tuned into our live [Vibe Check of Claude Cowork](https://every.to/vibe-check/vibe-check-claude-cowork-is-claude-code-for-the-rest-of-us) on X. We see the energy to stay at the edge of what’s possible, and we’re going all in on bringing you along for the ride.
[![Uploaded image](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/3892/optimized_61478328-c6a0-4b07-b061-49911da57e24.png)](https://every.to/courses/vibe-code-camp/)

On Thursday, January 22, we’re hosting **[Vibe Code Camp: All-day Edition](https://every.to/courses/vibe-code-camp/purchase)**, open to everyone. We’ve recruited the very best vibe coders in the world for a marathon livestream. **[Logan Kilpatrick](https://x.com/OfficialLoganK)**, **[Ryan Carson](https://x.com/ryancarson)**, **[Ben Tossell](https://x.com/bentossell)**, **[Tina He](https://x.com/fkpxls)**, **[Nat Eliason](https://x.com/nateliason)**, **[Ashe Magalhaes](https://x.com/ashebytes)** and more experts will join the Every team to show off in real time what they’re most excited about that didn’t exist even two months ago. We’ll follow up with step-by-step guides on how to take these demos and apply them as frameworks for your own work.
Then, on Friday, January 23, paid subscribers can join our first **[Agent-native Camp](https://every.to/courses/agent-native-camp)**, led by Dan. We’ll walk through agent-native architectures from first principles to working products. After watching experts operate at the frontier of the space, we’ll show you in-depth how agent-native software works and how to use it effectively.
Plus, there’s even more coming, and we don’t want you to miss out. From now until February 13, all new paid Every subscribers will get their first 30 days free. This is the perfect time to join: Use our speech-to-text tool Monologue to chat with Claude about agent-native software, join our Discord community of vibe coding obsessives, and get cutting-edge ideas daily as we explore this future in real time.
