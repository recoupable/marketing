# TLC Podcast #14 - What, Me Worry? Workshopping Dan Shipper

Rachel and Taylor join Dan to workshop his upcoming essay on something everyone has feelings about: Worry. Topics include: how we might change our thinking about our own thoughts, why we need a wider vocabulary to address issues like anxiety and depression, and why refusing yourself the time to worry can lead to action, not ignorance.
