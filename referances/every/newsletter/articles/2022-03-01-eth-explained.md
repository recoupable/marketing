# Ethereum Explained

*Preethi Kasireddy on what Ethereum is and how it works*

*You can watch this *[Means of Creation](https://pod.link/1527481453)* episode on *[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0YhF1jPDCW8)* or listen *[on your podcast app of choice](https://pod.link/1527481453/episode/dfa64678e7fa982a667c86bdf9849968)*.*
In this episode of our web3 explainer series, we take on an ambitious task: explaining Ethereum from the ground up. We unpack ***what*** Ethereum is, ***how*** it works, and ***why* **it matters.
We spoke to [Preethi Kasireddy](https://twitter.com/iam_preethi), founder of [DappCamp](https://www.dappcamp.xyz), which introduce students to the Ethereum ecosystem and helps them build their first web3 product in just three weeks.
Preethi is an entrepreneur, writer, engineer, and educator who started her crypto career as a software engineer at Coinbase. Her essay,“[How Does Ethereum Work Anyway](https://www.preethikasireddy.com/post/how-does-ethereum-work-anyway)”, is widely considered one of the best primers on Ethereum.  Her deep theoretical knowledge and practical experience makes her the perfect guest for this episode.
