# TLC Podcast #4: Writing as a Way of Being

Taking cues from Robert Yagelski’s [Writing as a Way of Being](https://www.dropbox.com/s/e2o1jqjwlqcjfn2/WritingasaWayofBeing.pdf?dl=0)*, *Rachel guides the panel through pedagogy. At the end of a taxing week, how can we access writing’s meditative potential? What myths have we been taught about “productive” writing — and how do we begin to unlearn them?
