Sometime around 2015 there was a mysterious vibe shift in web [design](https://every.to/c/design). It came so suddenly, and with so much decisive force, that it stood apart from the normal ebb and flow of aesthetic trends. It was like an invasive species taking over an ecosystem from a weaker competitor.
