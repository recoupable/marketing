## [GPT-4 Can Use Tools Now - That's a Big Deal](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/gpt-4-can-use-tools-now-that-s-a-big-deal)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_danshipper_an_AI_sitting_at_a_workbench_with_a_wall_of_tools_in_e2f5cd0b-6d41-499c-b2fc-87c6cbbefee2.png)](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/gpt-4-can-use-tools-now-that-s-a-big-deal)
*Dan Shipper / Chain of Thought *
GPT-4 took a giant leap forward this week with the introduction of function calling. This allows the AI to use interact with external code, databases, or APIs.
Dan Shipper explains the importance of this new development which makes GPT models more powerful and streamlined. He also examines how this update effects open source libraries in the ecosystem like Langchian. As AI continues to evolve, the competitive landscape and the way developers interact with language models will change dramatically.

## [The Satellite Renaissance](https://every.to/p/the-satellite-renaissance)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_upcLNq1o75GMK8q1L6SiO6qMP1WODsGlNjyB4UJ_oxdwu2iAiebmgGvMNRWsn-bmCbfQ7IVAlxQVq386nRcqz7XcVM4Tc8DRnaB4ok-NKdboa0cXoa6El3fByK3h.png)](https://every.to/p/the-satellite-renaissance)
*Anna-Sofia Lesiv *
The satellite renaissance is upon us, with over 7,000 active satellites now orbiting Earth, playing critical roles in navigation, weather monitoring, telecommunications, and more. Thanks in large part to SpaceX, entrepreneurs and innovators can now launch their own orbital machines.
In this insightful essay, Anna-Sofia Lesiv explores life during this satellite renaissance and the opportunities on the horizon, from space lasers providing more bandwidth to cleaning up space debris and commercial space science. As our ambitions in space grow, new challenges and technologies emerge, shaping the future of the space economy.

## What Students Made in Our Chatbot Course

Here are some of the most interesting and impressive projects that students created in our How to Build an AI chatbot course:

### [Book Talk](https://marketingmemetics.com/)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.10.05%20PM.png)](https://marketingmemetics.com/)
[By Michael Taylor](https://twitter.com/hammer_mt)
Michael built a bot for readers to talk to his book *Marketing Memetics *with cited sources.
[Try it](https://marketingmemetics.com/)

### [The Inside Scoop](https://the-inside-scoop.10xerai.repl.co/)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.13.32%20PM.png)](https://the-inside-scoop.10xerai.repl.co/)

[By Clement Lo](https://twitter.com/clementklo)
The Inside Scoop is a dining intelligence chatbot that helps you decide what to order at a restaurant. Select a restaurant and ask a question. The app will compose an answer based on recent reviews of the restaurant from Google, Yelp, and Tripadvisor.
[Try it](https://the-inside-scoop.10xerai.repl.co/)

### [Fluently](https://fluently.herokuapp.com/)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.16.49%20PM.png)](https://fluently.herokuapp.com/)

*By Artem Moskvin*
Fluently is a writing assistant that used AI to autocomplete your writing. It can help you write faster and improve your writing quality. This tool is particularly useful for non-native English speakers who may have difficulty finding the right words and phrases.
[Try it](https://fluently.herokuapp.com/)

### [PIE Bot](https://piebot.ai/signup)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.26.13%20PM.png)](https://piebot.ai/signup)

[By Henry Finkelstein](https://www.linkedin.com/in/henryfinkelstein/)
PIE Bot is focused on consolidating trustworthy information about psychedelics from multiple sources then synthesizing that knowledge into a coherent answer. I started with peer-reviewed science articles from reputable authors and journals plus well-regarded journalists in the industry. Over time, I'll train PIE Bot on more domains of thought such as ethnobotany, sociology, trip reports, and more.
[Try it](https://piebot.ai/signup)

### [Gen Z Translator](https://genz-translator.brownrygg.repl.co/)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.29.00%20PM.png)](https://genz-translator.brownrygg.repl.co/)

[By Brownrygg Woolls](https://twitter.com/aiupskillacademy)
A chatbot that lets you transform boring manager words into totally fetch Gen Z speak.
[Try it](https://genz-translator.brownrygg.repl.co/)

### AMA AI

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.22.14%20PM.png)](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2647/optimized_Screen%20Shot%202023-06-16%20at%203.22.14%20PM.png)

*By Matthew Thompson*
Discover the potential of AI-powered interactive dialogue with Ask Me Anything AI. This proof-of-concept project puts you in touch with your favorite creators and thinkers in an innovative way. Leveraging AI technology, AMA AI allows for unique, personalized interactions anytime you need guidance or inspiration. Imagine having access to the insights of your favorite thinkers whenever you want - whether you're brainstorming at 3 am or looking for immediate feedback on an idea. This is our initial step towards developing a platform that enhances the connection between creators and audiences.
