# Nat Eliason Joins Everything

[![](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/1255/optimized_82ea7f45-34c4-4033-802e-17ac7623f042_1600x800.png)](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/1255/optimized_82ea7f45-34c4-4033-802e-17ac7623f042_1600x800.png)

Hello Everything Members!

Big news: **[Nat Eliason](http://twitter.com/https://twitter.com/nateliason) is joining the bundle with a new paid newsletter. **It’s called [Almanack](https://every.to/thealmanack/), and its mission is to explore everything it takes for you to live a life that is healthy, wealthy, and wise.

You get access as part of your Everything subscription at no extra cost.

The first article is called *The Mental Model Behind Every High-Performer I Know*** **and [you can read it here.](https://every.to/thealmanack/the-mental-model-behind-every-high-10662024)

### Why we added Nat to the bundle

We’ve been admirers of Nat for a while, so over the last couple of months we asked him to write a few articles for us. He didn’t disappoint.

First, he wrote an article for Divinations called, *[Oatly: The New Coke](https://every.to/divinations/oatly-the-new-coke-10649354)*. To Nathan’s chagrin it immediately became the most read article ever in Divinations.

Nat followed up with a post for Superorganizers readers on [how he uses Roam to keep 250+ notes](https://every.to/superorganizers/how-i-took-notes-on-250-books-in-10649360). And the community loved it.

When we saw we’d found someone that has the range to write for *both* Superorganizers and Divinations we knew we’d found a good bet.

He’ll be writing two feature-length essays a month exploring how we can be healthier, more effective, and more successful, whatever our goals in life.

So whether you’re trying to build systems and processes to make your company run more smoothly, or you’re looking for tips on how to use Roam more effectively, or you want to know what the science says about what you should eat — Almanack will have something for you.

Curious? Read the first article now:

**[The Mental Model Behind Every High-Performer I Know](https://every.to/thealmanack/the-mental-model-behind-every-high-10662024)**

If you like what you read, make sure you [update your subscription settings](https://every.to/everything/) to make sure you get new emails from him.

### What else is going on in the bundle

We’re working hard to make your subscription valuable, and we’re launching new things all the time. Here are a few new additions to the bundle in case you missed them:

-
**Free Radicals** - Sherrell Dorsey interviews leaders in tech focusing on equity, advancement and progress.

-
**The Long Conversation** - a live weekly show celebrating the craft of writing hosted by Holloway’s Managing Editor Rachel Jepsen.

And these come in addition to what’s already available:

-
**Superorganizers** - Dan Shipper explores how the smartest people organize what they know to do their best work

-
**Praxis** - Tiago Forte explains his productivity systems like PARA, progressive summarization, and more.

-
**Divinations** - Nathan Baschez dives deep on business strategy and startups

-
**Means of Creation** - Li Jin and Nathan Baschez investigate the passion economy through the eyes of founders, investors, and creators

-
**Napkin Math** - Adam Keesling explores the intersection of investing and startups

Thank you so much for being a subscriber! We’d love to hear from you — please comment or leave feedback in the feedback form.

Best,
The Everything team
