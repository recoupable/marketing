# #48 - Consumable Software

Nathan loops Dan in on something he thought was a problem: falling in and out of love with different software too quickly. Turns out, it’s maybe not a problem at all—it’s the [kind of cycle we should lean into more often](https://every.to/superorganizers/productivity-cycles-294288). Listen through for how seeking novelty can help break monotony, and why you shouldn’t moralize about your own productivity.
