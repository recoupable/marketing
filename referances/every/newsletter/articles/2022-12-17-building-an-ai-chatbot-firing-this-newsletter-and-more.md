## [I Built an AI Chatbot Based On My Favorite Podcast](https://every.to/superorganizers/i-trained-a-gpt-3-chatbot-on-every-episode-of-my-favorite-podcast)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2415/optimized_danshipper_a_very_smart_chatbot_simultaneously_answering_questi_868e78e3-5518-4914-b220-b88c72f3c181-2.png)](https://every.to/superorganizers/i-trained-a-gpt-3-chatbot-on-every-episode-of-my-favorite-podcast)

*Dan Shipper / Superorganizers*
Dan is a huge fan of the [Huberman Lab podcast](http://hubermanlab.com/), a neuroscience podcast by Stanford neurobiologist Andrew Huberman. Unfortunately, the episodes are also very long—each one averages a few hours, and it's hard to find specific information by scrubbing through old episodes.
So he built a solution over the weekend: a *Huberman Lab* chatbot using GPT-3. You can ask it questions, and it will answer them based on transcripts from the show.
In this essay he unpacks how he built it, and what the implications are for the future of content creators.
[Read](https://every.to/superorganizers/i-trained-a-gpt-3-chatbot-on-every-episode-of-my-favorite-podcast)
