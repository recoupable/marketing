# So, We Raised Some Money

*$2 million co-led by Reid Hoffman and StartingLine VC*

*Was this newsletter forwarded to you? [Sign up](https://every.to/account) to get it in your inbox.*

---

Good news: We raised $2 million—an amount large enough that our investors will answer the phone if we need them, and small enough that we can keep being weird.
The round was co-led by [Reid Hoffman](https://every.to/podcast/how-to-prepare-for-agi-according-to-reid-hoffman) and [StartingLine VC](https://www.startingline.vc), with participation from [Will England](https://every.to/podcast/at-this-10-billion-hedge-fund-using-ai-just-became-mandatory) of Walleye Capital. It’s a unique structure: We're not taking it all at once; instead, we can draw it down when we need it. Call it a sip seed round.
AI helps us do more with less, so we don’t need a giant pile of cash. But a little money is nice, because it’ll let us try new experiments quickly and easily.
Now, we’ll get back to doing what we do best: Write and build at the frontier of AI.
You’ll see more of what you’ve come to expect from us. [Hands-on](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/vibe-check-codex-openai-s-new-coding-agent), [day-zero](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/vibe-check-o3-is-out-and-it-s-great) model reviews. Deep-dive essays on how AI [reshapes work](https://every.to/p/your-ceo-just-said-use-ai-or-else-here-s-what-to-do-next) and [the self](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/chatgpt-and-the-future-of-the-human-mind). AI-powered tools like [Cora](https://cora.computer), [Sparkle](https://makeitsparkle.co), and [Spiral](https://spiral.computer) that help you live and work better.
If you’re curious, you can read more about what we’re up to in the *[New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/05/21/business/media/ai-every-media-startup.html)* today.
Thanks for being along for the ride with us. I can’t wait for [what comes next](https://every.to/about).
Dan
