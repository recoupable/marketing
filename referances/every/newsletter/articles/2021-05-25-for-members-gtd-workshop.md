# For Members: GTD Workshop

We've been doing a bunch of events on our Every community Discord for paid members, and we have one coming up that I thought you might be interested in.
It's a 45-minute workshop on how to use David Allen's classic productivity system GTD. It's taught by my good friend [Khe Hy](https://twitter.com/khemaridh/) of [RadReads](https://radreads.co). Khe's one of the deepest productivity people that I know, and I'm extremely excited to refresh my memory on GTD and learn how to apply it in my life with him.

# Details

**Date: **Wednesday May 26th at 2:30 EST / 11:30 PST
**Where:** The Every Member's Discord.
**Description:**
Does every day feel like an uphill battle against non-stop tasks, meetings and emails? Nearly 20 years ago, David Allen promised us "mind like water" with his timeless *Getting Things Done *(GTD) philosophy. While often referenced, few professionals understand and implement this framework into their busy lives. Join RadReads creator Khe Hy for a 45-minute refresher on how you can get more done, and worry less with GTD.
