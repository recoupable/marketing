# Means of Creation #3: Lenny Rachitsky

*On newsletters, platforms, marketplaces, and more*

[![](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/1150/optimized_d72458bb-c8b1-4905-bdd3-2c89b2b7f711_2000x1000.png)](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/1150/optimized_d72458bb-c8b1-4905-bdd3-2c89b2b7f711_2000x1000.png)

I found out about Lenny’s newsletter at some point before the pandemic hit, I can’t remember exactly when. But I *do* remember how it made me feel.

*“This guy gets it!!”*

I had just recently left Substack and was thinking about what I wanted to do next, and here was this gift from the heavens, dropped in my inbox every week, with detailed, tactical advice on how to build things with people. I was instantly hooked.

Recently, Lenny took his newsletter paid, and it’s working incredibly well. He’s now #20 on the leaderboard of top paid publications.

Li and I are thrilled to have Lenny as our third guest for *Means of Creation*, our weekly talk show about the passion economy. We’d love for you to join us **tomorrow at 11am pacific / 2pm eastern** (same time every week!) to talk all things newsletters, platforms, marketplaces, and more.
