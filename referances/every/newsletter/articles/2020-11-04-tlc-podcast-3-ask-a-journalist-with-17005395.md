# TLC Podcast #3: Ask a Journalist, with John H. Richardson

The team talks to **John H. Richardson**, award-winning journalist for publications including *Esquire* and *GQ — *and Rachel’s dad! — about structure, note-taking, doing friendly battle with editors, and how and why to push through blank-page fears.
If you didn’t get a chance to read it before the live show last week, you can find “My Father the Spy” [here](https://www.esquire.com/news-politics/a1653/father-spy-0399/).
And check out John’s other articles mentioned in this episode — on [Dr. Willie Parker](https://classic.esquire.com/article/2014/9/1/the-abortion-ministry-of-dr-willie-parker); [the swinging doctor](https://www.gq.com/story/secret-life-americas-greatest-swinger); [prison theater](https://classic.esquire.com/article/2005/10/1/the-sing-sing-follies); and [polyamory](http://johnhrichardson.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-More-The-Merrier-Scenes-from-a-Group-Marriage-Part-2.pdf).
