# Introducing Spiral v2

*A totally redesigned and more powerful version of our tool to help you automate your repetitive creative work*

***TLDR:**** We’re launching a new version of Spiral—our tool to help you automate your repetitive creative work. It features a complete interface redesign and over 200+ Every-created Spirals to help you write everything from viral X posts, to LinkedIn posts, to blog posts. Find it *[on Product Hunt](https://www.producthunt.com/posts/spiral-9)*. Watch our launch video *[on X](https://x.com/danshipper/status/1861401545319031094)*. In June [we launched](https://every.to/p/introducing-spiral) [Spiral](https://spiral.computer/), a tool to help you automate your repetitive creative work.
Since then over 5,000 people have used Spiral more than 60,000 times to create viral X posts, product launch announcements, catchy headlines, YouTube descriptions, therapy notes, and SEC filing summaries—to name just a few examples.
Today we’re launching Spiral v2, a totally redesigned and more powerful version of Spiral.
Here’s what’s new:

- A complete interface redesign to help you filter and find Spirals easily for any channel or use case (we think it’s beautiful)

- 200 new Every-created Spirals that we’ve handcrafted to help you create incredible viral X posts, LinkedIn posts, blog posts, and more

- Significantly more powerful outputs with our enhanced AI writing engine and the new Claude Sonnet 3.5

You can now buy Spiral standalone for yourself or your team for $15 per seat per month. Of course, you can also get it as part of your Every subscription—along with access to all of our content and the software we build.

## What comes next

Our dream is to make Spiral a powerful tool to help you create content that’s catchy, confident, and authentic—that feels like the best version of you—in seconds. You can expect fast and frequent updates over the coming months to help you do exactly that:

-
**Content workflows**—we’ll help you turn one piece of content into outputs customized for many different channels, all in one click.

-
**Integrations**—we’ll make it easy to get inputs into Spiral from other tools and post outputs to your favorite channels, like LinkedIn, Slack, email, and more.

-
**More control and faster outputs**—we’ll make it easier to fine-tune Spiral output and see more results more quickly, so you can make progress as quickly as possible.
