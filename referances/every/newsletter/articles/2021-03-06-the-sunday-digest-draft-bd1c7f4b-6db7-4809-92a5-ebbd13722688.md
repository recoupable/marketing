## What’s Going On

#### [The End of Indie](https://medium.com/@bryce/the-end-of-indie-6e1b92d90b09)

In a [Medium post](https://medium.com/@bryce/the-end-of-indie-6e1b92d90b09) this week, mold-breaking VC **Indie** announced it was temporarily shuttering. Founded in 2015 inside of existing firm OATV, Indie was meant to diversify the kind of businesses that receive VC investments, including modest but sturdy companies, particularly ones with founders who were traditionally underrepresented in leadership positions. With close to 40 investments, founder Bryce Roberts wrote that the announcement was "not intended to be a pity party; rather, a public acknowledgment that the initial Indie experiment has come to an end."

#### NFTs From Several Angles

Depending where you fall on the Crypto-Adjacent Knowledge Spectrum (CAKS), you've been asking a different question about NFTs this week: [What the heck are they?](https://www.theverge.com/22310188/nft-explainer-what-is-blockchain-crypto-art-faq) [Are these prices ever going to chill?](https://www.cnbc.com/2021/03/04/mark-cuban-nft-pricing-will-settle-down-over-time.html)* *[Who paid 6 million dollars for Grimes' angelic art?](https://www.vanityfair.com/style/2021/03/grimes-art-sold-nft-cryptocurrency-auction-elon-musk-warnymph)* *
Of course, we covered the phenomenon brought on by this development for art-commerce and authenticity in [last week's Means of Creation roundup.](https://every.to/means-of-creation/can-nfts-be-a-new-monetisation-model-for-creators) But plenty more has happened since then, and we have some new favorite articles to make sense of it all. On the [a16z blog, Chris Dixon](https://a16z.com/2021/02/27/nfts-and-a-thousand-true-fans/) asserted that NFTMania means "the internet is trending back to" Kevin Kelly's *1000 True Fans *vision of "21st Century Patronage." [Sean Blanda](https://www.seanblanda.com/nft-for-writers/) thought through the potential process for writers to cash in on the craze as much as some artists have. And [Dark Star](https://darkstar.mirror.xyz/srmoGiN_1pg_toQGzCupkjWFOaf8xi0mM60zYpn_pwI) analyzed how NFTs will help "build a business on top of the creator economy."
