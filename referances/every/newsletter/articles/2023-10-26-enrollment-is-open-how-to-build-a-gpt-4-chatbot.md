## What’s the curriculum?

### Get Ready For the Most Creative Month of Your Life

**Kickoff (Nov 20): **A short live session with Dan to introduce you to the course and your fellow students.

**Week 1 (Nov 21 - Nov 28):** The basics of how GPT models work, how to code with ChatGPT, and use [Replit](https://www.replit.com/). You’ll build a basic chatbot in this first class.

**Week 2 (Nov 28 - Dec 5): **Why basic chatbots break and how to fix them, how to use [LllamaIndex](https://www.llamaindex.ai/) to build a chatbot that’s augmented with a knowledge base. You’ll learn the nuts and bolts of vector databases and embeddings.

**Week 3 (Dec 5 - Dec 12):** You’ll learn how to create a chatbot that you can interact with in a browser, how client/server interactions work, and how Flask can be used with Python to set up a web-based chatbot.

**Week 4 (Dec 12 - Dec 19):** How to make chatbots that use tools. You’ll learn how [Langchain](https://langchain.readthedocs.io/) and OpenAI function calling work and how to push your bot to production.

**Demo Day (Jan 12):** Demo your chatbot to your classmates and launch it on Every.
