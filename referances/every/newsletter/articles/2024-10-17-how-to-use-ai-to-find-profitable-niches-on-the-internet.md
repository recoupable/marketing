The internet is a crowded dataset of business ideas—for those with eyes to see them.

[Steph Smith](https://x.com/stephsmithio) and [Ben Tossell](https://x.com/bentossell) are using AI to discover the right opportunities and capitalize on them without burning through money or time. On [AI & I](https://every.to/podcast)* *, Dan Shipper interviewed the two, prompting them to articulate their thesis.

[Smith](https://every.to/podcast/the-internet-creator-s-guide-to-the-future) is the host of the [a16z Podcast](https://a16z.com/podcasts/a16z-podcast/) and the prolific internet creator behind [Internet Pipes](https://internetpipes.com/), a toolkit to unearth valuable insights on the internet. She also holds the distinction of being the only [repeat guest](https://every.to/podcast/how-to-find-your-next-big-idea-hiding-on-the-internet) on *AI & I*—so far, at least.

[Tossell](https://every.to/podcast/how-to-run-a-profitable-one-person-internet-business-using-ai) runs [Ben’s Bites](https://bensbites.beehiiv.com/), a daily AI newsletter with over 100,000 subscribers, and invests in early-stage AI startups. He’s also an experienced founder whose no-code platform [Makerpad](https://makerpad.zapier.com/) was [acquired](https://techcrunch.com/2021/03/08/zapier-buys-no-code-focused-makerpad-in-its-first-acquisition/) by Zapier. (Every recently [partnered](https://every.to/p/special-offer-subscribe-to-every-and-ben-s-bites-for-just-200) with Ben’s Bites to offer paid subscribers a bundle of AI writing, courses, and software.)

Read on for Smith and Tossell’s tips on running a scrappy, profitable internet business. We cover:

- How to find viable business ideas

- How to validate business ideas—quickly and cheaply

- How to bring your business ideas to life with AI

- The solo founder's toolkit
