# Tiago’s Life Goals

*I’ve decided to post my list of life goals here, after years of being cagey and secretive about them. My fear has been that people will somehow take advantage of me if these are known. But I’ve found that the more people know about what I’m trying to accomplish, the more ways they find to help me. I’ve kept this list for 13 years now, and it’s been a guide and a great motivator. I hope you gain something of value from it.*
