## [The Operating Manual for Your Nervous System](https://every.to/p/the-operating-manual-for-your-nervous-system)

[![](https://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/2507/optimized_AJo2HaezVuwsSTF3QW8W5x3VY2mTvOBEhz3ANt3P4rHbRauxAlcMgl8UbW3sqrObXCfqloeKpi2Et_M0t8VuxWtyM2h6mySh8OGIIVa1f5YO0KwicbMpA-OXoLO0.png)](https://every.to/p/the-operating-manual-for-your-nervous-system)

Your nervous system is a complex piece of hardware, but it's easier to control than you think.
