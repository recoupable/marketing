# #77 - Under Talk Therapy's hood, with guest host Freia Lobo

Dan and Nathan are joined by Talk Therapy's inaugural guest host, Every community member (and investor!) Freia Lobo—who's also an avid listener. Answering Freia's questions about their podcast's near-yearlong journey, the cohosts and cofounders discuss whether there are boundaries for on-air discussions, why the pod has become an integral part of their working mental health, and what they hope the business community at large might take away from their show.
