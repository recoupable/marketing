# Means of Creation Community Hangout with Li Jin and Nathan Baschez

*A special episode with your Means of Creation hosts*

This week on Means of Creation we did something a bit different: a casual Q&A and hangout with us, Li Jin and Nathan Baschez!
There’s a budding community here at Means of Creation and we wanted to spend more time with everyone. So for this week, we asked you to **submit questions **and we answered them live with you! This was anything related to the show or the Passion Economy in general. Examples include:

- Questions about **specific companies or industries**

-
**Pitch us your startup** or idea!

- Questions about **news events**

- Things we work on **outside of Means of Creation**

We’ll also touched on some of the [news this week](https://every.to/meansofcreation/news-roundup-8-could-the-trump-bump-18176871) and answered questions from past guests that we couldn’t get to at the time.
