# A new collaboration with Jerry Colonna

Ask Jerry will be a newsletter series produced by me, [Andy Sparks](https://twitter.com/SparksZilla) and the team at[Everything](https://every.to/everything/), featuring[Jerry Colonna](https://twitter.com/jerrycolonna), author of [Reboot: Leadership and the Art of Growing Up](https://www.amazon.com/Reboot-Leadership-Art-Growing-Up-ebook/dp/B0796SNDSK), and CEO of executive coaching and leadership development firm[Reboot](https://www.reboot.io/). Since founding Reboot in 2014, Jerry has developed a reputation for helping leaders grow through what he calls “radical self-inquiry,” and he is one of the most sought after coaches in the world of startups.

In this series, Jerry and I will discuss questions about psychological habits, behavioral patterns, and the inner demons that can lead people to succeed—yet can be detrimental to their relationships and well-being.
