How do you make sure you’re in touch with the people who matter the most to you?

It’s hard — I’ve tried a bunch of different systems. And it’s something we’ve seen come up in a lot of Superorganizers interviews. Our interviews with VCs [Cam Porter](https://every.to/superorganizers/how-a-pro-soccer-player-turned-vc-294323), and [Maria Salamanca come to mind](https://every.to/superorganizers/one-vc-wrote-an-os-for-her-life-294333).

They’ve both built elaborate systems to help them keep in touch with the people who matter most to them. So we used them as inspiration, and built a Notion template to help you maintain your closest friendships.

It’s called *A Friendly Reminder*.

## How does it work?

A Friendly Reminder is a Notion template built to keep you in touch with the people who matter most.

[![](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/933/optimized_d1aec42b-4ae3-467e-a582-27e249a3eeed_1335x547.png)](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/933/optimized_d1aec42b-4ae3-467e-a582-27e249a3eeed_1335x547.png)

All you have to do is tell it when you last talked to someone, the frequency that you want to reach out to them in the future, and the template will take care of reminding you who you need to talk to.

## Multiple Views: Full, Contact Soon, Overdue

We wanted it to be easy and quick to figure out who you need to reach out to, so we built three views: full view, contact soon, and overdue.

The full view contains all your friends.

## Location views

Ever get to a city and wonder who you should see? This template makes it easy to see your friends grouped by city. It comes with two views built in: New York and San Francisco. But you can easily add as many as you need.

[![](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/933/optimized_3826b97f-f8e5-4a03-acfa-cbb36d0c0d7c_1470x455.png)](http://d24ovhgu8s7341.cloudfront.net/uploads/editor/posts/933/optimized_3826b97f-f8e5-4a03-acfa-cbb36d0c0d7c_1470x455.png)

## Ideas for meetups

We even took it a step further by helping you schedule the type of meeting and where you could meetup. Sometimes the biggest barrier to talking to a friend is having a fun and convenient spot to meet at.

The best way to do this is to list any place you’ve been with someone. That way, it’s easy to meet there again. For some variety, you can borrow locations you went to with other friends!

## How to personalize it

Step 1: add your friends. For the name, you can type them manually or [import](https://www.notion.so/notion/Import-data-into-Notion-18c37b470e8941789548b68049af750b) them from another source.

Step 2: update the “Last Connected” and “Remind me after…” columns. These two columns power the rest of the template.

Step 3: keep in touch with your friends! For best results, add a few notes after each conversation.

That’s it! This template works if you review it regularly.
