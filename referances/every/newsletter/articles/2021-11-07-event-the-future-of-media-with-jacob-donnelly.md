# Event: The Future of Media with Jacob Donnelly

*Join us on the Every Discord on Thursday, November 11th at 12 PM EST / 9 AM PST*

[Jacob Donnelly](https://twitter.com/JayCoDon), GM of B2B at [Morning Brew](https://morningbrew.com) and writer of the [A Media Operator](http://amediaoperator.com) newsletter, is coming to the Every Discord to teach you about the future of media on the internet. Join us live on Thursday, November 11th at 12 PM EST / 9 AM PST to talk about:

- How to be a successful solo writer while also holding down a demanding full-time job

- What he’s working on at Morning Brew and how he sees the media landscape evolving for newsletters

- How writers and publications should think about ads vs. subscriptions

- What he thinks about media bundles like Every

And more! We’ll also have time for a Q&A so you’ll be able to ask Jacob any questions you have directly.

### Who is Jacob Donnelly?

Jacob is the GM of B2B at Morning Brew, which means he’s responsible for building their vertical newsletter offerings like HR Brew, Marketing Brew, and Retail Brew. He’s also the writer of [A Media Operator](http://amediaoperator.com)—a newsletter about the media business read by all of the top operators in media.
