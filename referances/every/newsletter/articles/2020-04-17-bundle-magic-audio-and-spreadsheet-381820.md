# Bundle Magic audio & spreadsheet

Happy Friday!

Above is a recording of me narrating this week’s essay explaining bundle economics, “[Bundle Magic](https://every.to/divinations/bundle-magic-379035).” I tried something a little different than usual on the back half. Instead of just reading the text, I riff off of it. I got a little feedback that I’m not the most talented narrator (fair!) so I thought I’d give this a shot. The style is inspired by Dan Carlin’s wonderful “Hardcore History.” Does it work?

If you haven’t already, I encourage you to add the RSS feed to your podcast app! Just click the “listen in podcast app” link by the audio player above.
