# 🎧 How Packy McCormick Finds His Next Big Idea

*One of the most influential voices in tech explains how AI helps him write and invest*

*TL;DR: Today we’re releasing a new episode of our podcast *AI & I.* I go in depth with ****Packy McCormick****, writer of *[Not Boring](https://www.notboring.co/)*, a newsletter about technology and startups, and founder of *[Not Boring Capital](https://www.notboring.co/p/introducing-not-boring-capital)*, an early-stage fund that backs ambitious startups. ****Watch on ***[X](https://x.com/danshipper/status/1821161412367884367)*** or ***[YouTube](https://youtu.be/AcgrFWKQdoQ)***, or listen on ***[Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/2RJIpwLbEBK4XgAG5Y0oS4)*** or ***[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/how-packy-mccormick-finds-his-next-big-idea-ep-29/id1719789201?i=1000664599024)***.***

[Packy McCormick](https://x.com/packyM)’s job is to find, articulate, and invest in the next big idea. I spent an hour with him to understand how he’s baked AI into his process.

Packy writes [Not Boring](https://www.notboring.co/), a newsletter that analyzes technology and startups for 200,000 subscribers every week. He also invests through his fund, [Not Boring Capital](https://www.notboring.co/p/introducing-not-boring-capital), which backs early-stage companies that combine sci-fi ideas with viable business models. And he’s an advisor at [a16z crypto](https://a16zcrypto.com/).

We talked about why Packy thinks vertically integrated businesses are the future of technology, how he uses Claude’s [Projects](https://www.anthropic.com/news/projects) feature to edit his newsletter, and the process behind making interactive graphics to illustrate complex concepts from his essays. We also discussed the tools he uses to research, write, and edit essays—plus, we got his hot takes on the crypto market. To cap it off, we used Projects to build a custom AI tool that grades and reviews Packy’s essays live on the show. This is a must-watch for writers, investors, and anyone trying to understand the cutting edge of technology.
