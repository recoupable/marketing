# Every — every.to

AI media company founded by Dan Shipper. "The Only Subscription You Need to Stay at the Edge of AI." 100,000 builders; 130K+ newsletter subscribers; clients representing $175B+ AUM; 16K+ GitHub stars on open-source work.

**The model — one flywheel, four surfaces:**
1. **Media** — daily newsletter + columns (Chain of Thought, Context Window, Vibe Check model reviews on release day) + "AI & I" podcast
2. **Products** — a studio of AI apps bundled into the subscription: Cora (email), Spiral (writing), Sparkle (file organization), Monologue (dictation), Proof (human+AI collaborative docs), Plus One (AI agent in Slack); built with entrepreneurs-in-residence
3. **Consulting** — "Transform Your Company With AI... from the team that writes the playbooks, ships the products, and trains top teams" (Disney, Ogilvy, Lionsgate, FAA)
4. **Events** — virtual "Power User Camps" (Fable 5 Camp, Codex Camp, Compound Engineering Camp), IRL events, executive sessions

Their own words (products page): "our writing, podcasts, products, and consulting projects are a positive feedback loop: All explore similar ideas about the future in different ways that, in turn, help improve the other."

**Why it's in references:**
1. **The bundle thesis** — content + apps in ONE subscription. The paywall popup literally lists apps as a subscriber benefit. This is the mature version of what pm-world does solo and a different answer than Tenex's services-only play.
2. **Editorial design at its best** — literary-magazine aesthetic (serif wordmark, swash italics, vintage-collage illustration system) applied to an AI company. See `DESIGN.md`.
3. **Copy that sells a feeling of velocity** — "stay at the edge," "What Comes Next," model reviews on release day. See `COPY.md`.
4. **Consulting credibility loop** — "Work with builders, not followers": we write it, we ship it, we open-source it, therefore hire us. See `TEARDOWN.md`.
5. **Compound Engineering** — their open-source plugin (7,000+ GitHub stars, "endorsed by the creator of Claude Code") is the proof-of-expertise engine. We already use their compound-engineering skills in this monorepo.
