# Tenex — tenex.co

AI transformation consultancy. "Your AI transformation partner. We set & execute your enterprise AI strategy at startup speed."

**Who's behind it:** Alex Lieberman (co-founder of Morning Brew — $75M exit to Business Insider, also Storyarb/Distro/growthpair) and Arman Hezarkhani (ex-Google Cloud/AI scaling, CMU adjunct CS professor, multiple venture-backed AI companies) as Managing Partners. Calls itself "the fastest growing AI transformation company."

**What they sell:**
- **AI Transformation** — enterprise strategy + execution across three pillars: product, process, people. "We are your Chief AI Officer."
- **AI Engineering** — outcome-based engineering squads. Priced on story points delivered, not hours logged.

**How they sell it:** Media-company-style content engine (the Morning Brew playbook applied to consulting):
- **Playbooks** — YouTube-style video guides with clickbait-craft thumbnails ("Build an AI Agent That Replaces 90% of Your Sales Team")
- **ultrathink** — weekly newsletter, 40,000+ execs/builders, "your unfair advantage in the AI arms race"
- Both funnel to a qualification form (annual revenue dropdown → sales call)

1. **Design** — the strongest "classical meets AI" brand language we've captured: black/yellow/white, B&W Greek statues using modern tech, pixel-serif display type. Overlaps with Recoup's existing pixel-font direction (Geist Pixel Square). See `DESIGN.md`.
2. **Copy** — historical-stakes narrative ("Win the next decade," "Stay on the right side of history"), binary disrupt-or-be-disrupted framing, anti-consultancy positioning. See `COPY.md`.
3. **Funnel** — the enterprise-tier complement to the pm-world self-serve playbook (`../pm-world/TEARDOWN.md`): same teach-publicly motion, but content → qualification form → high-ticket services instead of content → $99 zip. Recoup spans both tiers. See `TEARDOWN.md`.
