## Blog Intro (Approved ✅)

> Last Tuesday I sat down at 10pm to make content for Gatsby Grace.
> By midnight I had 22 finished videos. Captioned. Formatted. Queued.
> I didn't edit a single one.

**WHY THIS WORKS:** Opens with a specific moment ("Last Tuesday, 10pm"). Shows, doesn't tell — the reader discovers the scale (22 videos) through the story, not a claim. No marketing language. No adjectives. The last line ("I didn't edit a single one") is the payoff — it reframes what "22 videos" means. The reader leans in because it sounds like a real person sharing a real experience.

## Social Post — LinkedIn (Approved ✅)

> I used to spend 3 hours making one Instagram post for my artist.
>
> Last week, an AI agent made 22 videos in 2 hours.
> Captioned. Platform-formatted. Scheduled.
>
> This isn't "AI art." This is AI-driven marketing.
> The artist's voice stays. The grind goes.

**WHY THIS WORKS:** Hook line creates contrast (3 hours → 22 videos). Specific numbers build credibility. "This isn't 'AI art'" directly addresses the biggest objection. Ends with a clear value statement in the reader's own language ("the grind goes").

## Homepage Headline (Approved ✅)

> Your AI marketing team — always on, always creating, always learning.

**WHY THIS WORKS:** "Your" makes it personal. Three parallel phrases create rhythm. "Always learning" differentiates from static tools. No jargon. Under 15 words.

## Bad Example (Rejected ❌)

> Recoupable is a revolutionary AI platform that leverages cutting-edge
> technology to transform the music industry!

**WHY REJECTED:** "Revolutionary" — empty superlative. "Leverages" — corporate jargon (say "uses"). "Cutting-edge" — cliché that signals nothing new to say. "Transform the music industry" — grandiose claim with no proof. Exclamation mark — shouting because the words aren't doing the work. Violates every rule in voice.md.

---

## Bad Example (Rejected ❌)

> In today's fast-paced digital landscape, artists need innovative solutions
> to stay competitive. Recoupable offers a comprehensive suite of AI-powered
> tools designed to maximize your marketing potential.

**WHY REJECTED:** "In today's fast-paced..." — the most generic opening in AI-generated content. "Innovative solutions" — says nothing specific. "Comprehensive suite" — corporate catalog language. "Maximize your marketing potential" — what does this even mean? A reader learns nothing from this paragraph. Compare to the approved intro — by the end of three lines, you know exactly what happened and why it matters.
