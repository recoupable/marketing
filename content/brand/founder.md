# Founder Story — Sidney Swift

> Updated: 2026-03-16

## Who

Sidney Swift — founder of Recoupable. Builder, musician, and AI-curious technologist who started asking: "What if an AI could handle the marketing grind for artists?"

## The Origin

Sidney experienced the content grind firsthand. Creating social content, formatting for each platform, posting consistently — it consumed hours that could have been spent making music. The insight wasn't "let's build another social tool." It was: "What if an AI agent could own the entire marketing workflow — not just one step?"

## The Gatsby Grace Experiment

The proof point that validated everything. Sidney created Gatsby Grace — an AI-driven artist persona — and used Recoupable to run her entire content pipeline:

- 22 finished videos in one session
- Captioned, formatted, and queued — zero manual editing
- Content posted across Instagram, TikTok, and X
- All driven by AI agents reading context files about the artist

This wasn't a demo. It was a working system. And it proved that AI agents could handle music marketing at a quality and speed that wasn't possible manually.

## Story Arc (for thought leadership content)

1. **The problem** — Artists spend more time marketing than making music
2. **The failed solutions** — Schedulers, templates, and tools that still require hours of work
3. **The insight** — An AI agent, not a tool, that acts autonomously with context
4. **The proof** — Gatsby Grace: 22 videos, zero editing, full pipeline
5. **The mission** — Give every artist an AI marketing team

## Milestones

- Built Recoupable platform (chat + API + tasks infrastructure)
- Gatsby Grace experiment: validated AI content pipeline end-to-end
- CLI tool published to npm (`recoup` command)
- Skills system: modular AI agent capabilities
- Building in public — sharing the journey as it happens
