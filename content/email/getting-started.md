# Getting Started Email

**Type:** Semi-automated (personalize if replying to a thread, template if not)
**Trigger:** Day 2 of trial (skip if user already replied to welcome email)
**Goal:** Get them to their first "aha" moment — seeing real data about their artist.
**Sequence:** 2 of N (see `sequence-overview.md`)

---

## Template

**Subject:** 3 ways to get started with Recoupable

Hey [Name or 👋],

Quick follow-up — wanted to make sure you know the fastest ways to get value out of Recoupable:

**1. Ask the agent to onboard you**
Open a chat at chat.recoupable.com and type something like "help me get started" or "run onboarding." The agent will walk you through setting up your first artist and pulling research.

**2. Add an artist and run a research report**
Head to chat.recoupable.com, add an artist to your roster, and ask the agent to research them. You'll get back audience insights, streaming data, and actionable recommendations.

**3. Email the agent directly**
Send a message to agent@recoupable.com with your artist's name and what you want to know. It works like chatting, but from your inbox.

Any of these will get you going in under 5 minutes. If you get stuck on any of them, just reply here.

-- Sidney

---

## Design Decisions

- **"3 ways" in subject line** — sets expectations, feels scannable, not overwhelming
- **Onboarding option listed first** — lowest friction for users who don't know where to start
- **Email agent option listed last** — alternative path for people who prefer inbox over web apps
- **"Under 5 minutes"** — reduces perceived effort
- **Skip if they replied to email 1** — if they're already talking to you, this is redundant
