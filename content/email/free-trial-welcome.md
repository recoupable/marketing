# Free Trial Welcome Email

**Type:** Manual, personal outreach from founder
**Trigger:** New free trial signup
**Send timing:** Day 0 — within 24 hours of signup
**Goal:** Establish Sidney as a real person. Pure warmth, no agenda.
**Sequence:** 1 of N (see `sequence-overview.md`)

---

## Template

**Subject:** Yooooo

Hey [Name or 👋]

Saw you started a free trial on Recoupable.

LMK if you need any help getting started.

We're still in beta so pardon our dust.

But LOTS of amazing new features.

And tons more coming SOON!

-- Sidney

---

## Design Decisions

- **"Yooooo" subject line** — impossible to mistake for an automated email. Gets opened.
- **No agenda** — no questions, no CTAs, no onboarding checklist. Just a human saying hey.
- **Short, punchy lines** — reads like a text message, not a marketing email
- **"LMK if you need any help"** — plants the seed that help is available without pushing it
- **"Pardon our dust" into "LOTS of amazing new features"** — acknowledges beta reality but immediately pivots to excitement
- **Sets up email 2 and 3** — once they know Sidney is a real person, the follow-up emails (getting started, personalized research) land harder
