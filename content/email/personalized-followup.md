# Personalized Follow-Up Email

**Type:** Manual, personal outreach from founder
**Trigger:** Day 5 of trial
**Goal:** Show you did your homework on them. Earn a reply. Learn what they need.
**Sequence:** 3 of N (see `sequence-overview.md`)

---

## Template

**Subject:** Quick question about [company/artist name]

Hey [Name or 👋]

I took a look at [something about their company] and [genuine observation]. Really cool to see what you're building.

Is there anything specific that Recoupable or I can help with to make sure you get the most out of your free trial?

-- Sidney

---

## Example: Just Push Play

**Subject:** Quick question about Just Push Play

Hey 👋

I took a look at Just Push Play and am a big fan of what Giampaolo is doing with AI music. Really cool to see what you're building.

Is there anything specific that Recoupable or I can help with to make sure you get the most out of your free trial?

-- Sidney

---

## How to Personalize

Spend 30 seconds looking at their company/artists before sending. Fill in:

- **[something about their company]** — their site, roster, Spotify catalog, social presence
- **[genuine observation]** — something true you noticed (e.g. "big fan of what [person] is doing with [thing]")

## Design Decisions

- **Comes after the casual welcome and getting-started emails** — by day 5 they already know who Sidney is, so the personalized research feels like a natural deepening of the relationship, not a cold sales tactic
- **Short and focused** — just the observation + one question. Nothing else competing for attention.
- **"Recoupable or I"** — "Recoupable" surfaces why they signed up; "or I" signals a real human will personally help
- **Question is easy to answer** — "is there anything specific" can be yes/no or a quick sentence
